About us

Laneth Tech Company Limited, established in August 2023, specializes in Core Banking Solutions tailored for microfinance institutions, rural banks, and credit unions worldwide. Additionally, our company extends support to Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs). Our resolute team, consisting of both foreign and Thai experts, possesses extensive experience in software development, testing, and support within the financial software domain, particularly in microfinance and rural banking. Our primary objective is to offer a highly scalable system suitable for both small institutions and large organizations. We provide a diverse range of applications, including Loans, Leasing, Savings, Current accounts, Time Deposits, and Shares. Notably, our system is exceptionally configurable, allowing for fully customizable products across various applications and a broad spectrum of account settings. We adhere to modern financial operation standards, facilitating seamless integration with other systems through RESTful APIs for mobile and internet banking apps, and ISO 20022 for money transfer gateways such as SEPA, SWIFT, or similar protocols. We pride ourselves on offering a cost-effective solution, targeting numerous smaller organizations. Currently, our focus is on expanding digital services, such as OCR services for simplified client data capture verifications, QR Code transfers, and payment facilities. Additionally, we are actively working on integrating Know Your Customer (KYC) processes and the Loan Application system.
